milkshake is a very delicious drink, it is loved all over the world

crazyshakes

Ingredients

  • vanilla ice cream
  • milk
  • cocoa
  • strawberries

    • STEPBYSTEP COOKING RECIPE

    Step 1

    Place the ice cream in the bowl of a blender or food processor. Pour in the milk and beat for 1-2 minutes. Divide the milkshake tail into three equal parts. Leave one piece

    Step 2

    in blender, add strawberries and blend again. To another part add cocoa and stir. Leave the third as is.

    Step 3

    Pour the cocktail in layers into two tall glasses, garnish whipped cream and multi-colored toppings, add berries strawberries and serve immediately.
